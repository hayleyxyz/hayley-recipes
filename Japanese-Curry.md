# Japanese curry

## Ingredients
- 1 Large leek
- Chicken, Lamb or Beef (450g+)
- Potatoes
- Carrots
- Curry roux
- Onion
- 2-3 Garlic cloves
- Virgin or Refined Olive oil
- Worcestershire sauce
- Garlic (optional)
- Herbs: Thyme, Rosemary, Sage, Oregano etc. (optional)

## Method

- Cut the onion, and mince the garlic cloves.
